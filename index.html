1 OR 2

How to Play Welcome to 1 or 2! This is game where you have to guess a number between 1 and 2.

The game is simple but a little hard. There are two numbers, 1 and 2. you start off the game by selecting a goal number between 2 and 10. Then you should select a number between 1 and 2 for the opponent to guess. If the opponent guesses it correctly, it gets a point, otherwise you get a point. Then the opponent selects a random number between 1 and 2 and you have to guess it and its the same point giving system. The game continues like this until you or your opponent reaches the goal number. You can play this game with a bot or with another friend. Good luck!
